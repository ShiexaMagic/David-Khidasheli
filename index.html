About the Artist

David Khidasheli is a Georgian artist whose work celebrates the rich traditions of Georgian decorative painting while embracing a deeply personal artistic vision.

Working primarily in oil on canvas, his paintings feature lush botanical compositions, serene landscapes, and expressive animal portraits. Each piece showcases his distinctive style — rich textures achieved through layered brushwork, warm earthy palettes punctuated by vivid florals, and a decorative sensibility that transforms everyday subjects into something timeless.

Every painting is a unique, handcrafted original — there are no prints or reproductions. When you purchase a work, you receive a one-of-a-kind piece of Georgian art.
