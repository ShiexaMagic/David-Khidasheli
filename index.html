About the Artist

David Khidasheli is a Georgian painter from Tbilisi. He works with egg yolk tempera and natural pigments on board — a traditional technique that gives each painting a distinctive matte warmth and depth of colour.

His paintings range from lush botanical still lifes and quiet Georgian landscapes to expressive animal portraits and angel compositions. The style is unmistakably his own — layered brushwork that builds rich texture, a warm earthy palette brought to life by vivid flowers, and a decorative sensibility that turns everyday subjects into something timeless.

Every painting is a unique, handmade original — no prints, no reproductions. When you acquire a work, you receive a one-of-a-kind piece of Georgian art made with centuries-old methods.
